How to plant a tree in eight simple steps

  1. Dig a hole. Make sure it's the same depth as the tree's rootball but three times as wide.
  2. Soak the rootball.
  3. Check the rootball.
  4. Position your tree.
  5. Refill the hole.
  6. Add a tree guard or spiral.
  7. Water well and mulch.
  8. Insert a sturdy stake.
